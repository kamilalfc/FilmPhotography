35mm film adventure Canon AE-1 Program

For my first Film Camera I chose one of the most popular cameras of all time - Canon AE-1 Program with FD 50mm f/1.8 lens and I love everything about it! Super easy to use and exposure control is a dream what makes this camera beginners' best choice. Canon AE-1 Program is a straightforward electronic camera that can shoot in manual, fully automatic Program mode, and semi-automatic Shutter-Priority mode.

Favourite 35mm film stocks

My favourite 35mm film is definately Kodak Portra 400. This film does a fantastic job of reproducing a natural skin tone color and can work well even in low light. Kodak 400 is known for its high color saturation and low contrast. Currently I'm experimenting with Kodak Color Plus 200, Kodak Ultramax 400 and FujiFilm c200.
